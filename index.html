KISII MESSIANIC STUDENTS ASSOCIATION

Preamble

We the members of the church of God believe in 40 points of doctrine which have been prepared and cover the principle teachings of the church. These 40 points of believes represent lifetimes and years of the study of scriptures on the path of dedicated men of God who devoted their lives to “rightly dividing the word of truth”. They are and have been the teachings of the church of God for generations, and are in harmony with the doctrines as taught by Christ and the apostles in the early days of the church. All these points of believe have been taken directly from the Bible, God’s holy word, which is our only guide and scriptural references are given after each point. Attached in within the constitution are the 40 points of doctrine. We adopt enact and give this constitution to ourselves and future kisii University messianic students association. (KSUMSA)

Our Mission

A. To be good ambassadors of Christ while in the university by ensuring that we live a life that would be a good example to others; a life of consecration unto God.
B. To promote individual’s self-development through encouraging, challenging, and correcting each other.

Our Vision

a. Promoting exposure of members to the word of God.
b. Providing an environment where members can increase in wisdom and understanding in favor with God and man.
c. Participate in activities that will strengthen our faith. Providing members with lessons and other church materials.
